Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about social00.

Are all these tools really free?

Yes — every single one of the 100 tools on social00 is completely free. There are no hidden fees, premium tiers or paywalls. We believe creators deserve access to powerful tools without paying a fortune.

Do I need to create an account or login?

No account required at all. Simply visit the tool you need and start using it immediately. We designed social00 to have zero friction — no email, no password, no verification.

Can I use these tools on my mobile phone?

Absolutely. social00 is built mobile-first and works perfectly on all smartphones, tablets and desktop computers. The interface adapts to your screen size automatically.

How often are new tools added?

We release new tools every week based on creator requests and trends. Subscribe to our newsletter to get notified whenever a new tool drops. We're constantly building based on what creators actually need.

Are the AI-generated results accurate?

Our AI tools use state-of-the-art language models to generate high-quality outputs. Results are excellent starting points that you can refine to match your personal style. The more specific your input, the better the output.

Can I use the generated content commercially?

Yes. All content generated using social00 tools is yours to use however you like — for personal projects, client work, YouTube channels, social media and commercial purposes.

Is my data safe when I use these tools?

We take your privacy seriously. We do not store any content you input into the tools. Sessions are temporary and no personal data is retained. Please review our Privacy Policy for full details.
